About the club

Built in Amman.
By the people who
build the buildings.

KNX Jordan Club is a peer-elected society of certified integrators, consultants and educators working across the Kingdom. We don’t sell hardware and we don’t speak for any manufacturer — members govern the bylaws, set the certification ladder, and review every applicant by hand.

Peer review, not pay-to-play

Every application is reviewed by three sitting members. References are checked. Project work is sampled.

Vendor-neutral by charter

No single manufacturer holds more than 4% of voting weight. Sponsorship is published quarterly.

A standing certification ladder

Apprentice · Practitioner · Professional · Tutor · Fellow. Continuing-education credits required annually.

Local chapters, global standards

42 chapters meet monthly. The board ratifies a single global handbook every two years.

Member directory

A searchable network of verified specialists.
